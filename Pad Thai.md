# Pad Thai

**Serves:** 4

## Ingredients

- 8 oz flat rice noodles
- 3 tbsp oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 8 oz uncooked shrimp, chicken, or extra-firm tofu
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 1 cup fresh bean sprouts
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- 3 green onions, chopped
- 1/2 cup dry roasted peanuts
- 2 limes, in wedges
- 1/2 cup fresh cilantro

### Pad Thai sauce

- 3 tbsp fish sauce
- 1 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce
- 5 tbsp light brown sugar
- 2 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp Sriracha
- 2 tbsp creamy peanut butter

## Instructions

1. Cook noodles per package directions, just until tender. Rinse under cold water.
2. Whisk sauce ingredients in a bowl. Set aside.
3. Heat 1 1/2 tbsp oil in a wok or large pan over medium-high. Add the protein, garlic, and bell pepper. Cook shrimp 1–2 minutes per side until pink, or chicken 3–4 minutes until cooked through.
4. Push everything to the side. Add a little more oil and the eggs. Scramble, breaking into small pieces.
5. Add noodles, sauce, bean sprouts, and most of the peanuts. Toss to combine.
6. Top with green onions, remaining peanuts, cilantro, and lime wedges. Serve immediately.
