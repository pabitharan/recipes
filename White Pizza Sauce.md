# White Pizza Sauce

**Makes:** 1 1/2 cups (enough for 4 ten-inch pizzas) · **Time:** 5 min

## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp unsalted butter
- 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
- 1 1/4 cups milk (2% or whole)
- 1/4 tsp sea salt
- 1/8 tsp black pepper
- 2 cloves garlic, pressed or finely grated
- 1/4 cup shredded parmesan

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a small saucepan over medium. Whisk in flour and keep whisking 1–2 minutes — don't let it brown.
2. Slowly add milk, whisking constantly, until smooth, thickened, and bubbly.
3. Whisk in salt, pepper, and garlic.
4. Add parmesan and whisk 30 seconds. Pull from the heat and keep whisking just until cheese is melted. Don't boil after adding cheese or it may curdle.
5. Cool before using. Refrigerate up to 5 days.
