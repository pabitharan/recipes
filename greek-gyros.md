# Homemade Greek Gyros

**Serves:** 8 · **Prep:** 20 min · **Cook:** 1 hr 15 min

## Ingredients

- 1 onion
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 lb ground lamb
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 tbsp dried marjoram
- 1 tbsp dried rosemary
- 2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper

### To serve

- 1 cup tzatziki sauce
- 8 leaves lettuce
- 16 tomato slices
- 8 slices sweet onion
- 8 pita breads

## Instructions

1. Pulse the onion in a food processor about 15 seconds. Place in a kitchen towel and squeeze out the juice (discard juice). Return onion pulp to the processor.
2. Add meat, garlic, marjoram, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Process until it looks like a fine paste, about 1 minute.
3. Press the mixture evenly into a loaf pan.
4. Set the loaf pan inside a 9×13 baking pan. Fill the larger pan with water to make a water bath. Bake at 325°F about 1 hour 20 minutes, until the meat reaches 170°F internal.
5. Drain liquid from the loaf pan and let the meat rest, covered with foil, 15 minutes.
6. Slice as thinly as possible (an electric knife works best).
7. *Optional but recommended:* Add slices to a nonstick pan with a little olive oil and brown for crispy edges.
8. Warm pita. Fill each with meat, tzatziki, lettuce, tomato, and sweet onion.
