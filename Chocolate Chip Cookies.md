# Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies

**Makes:** 12 cookies · **Prep:** 20 min · **Cook:** 15 min

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 3/4 cup brown sugar, packed
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
- 1 large egg
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 4 oz milk or semi-sweet chocolate chunks
- 4 oz dark chocolate chunks (or your preference)

## Instructions

1. Whisk sugars, salt, and melted butter into a smooth paste with no lumps.
2. Whisk in the egg and vanilla until light ribbons fall off the whisk and hold briefly.
3. Sift in flour and baking soda. Fold with a spatula — don't overmix or the cookies will turn out cakey.
4. Fold in chocolate chunks. Chill at least 30 minutes, or overnight for deeper flavor.
5. Heat oven to 350°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment.
6. Scoop dough with an ice cream scoop, leaving 4 inches between cookies.
7. Bake 12–15 minutes, until edges are barely browned.
8. Cool completely before serving.
