# Rapini and Potatoes

**Serves:** 4

## Ingredients

- 1 bunch rapini, chopped
- 3 potatoes, peeled and chopped
- 1/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 red chile pepper, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, peeled and chopped
- 1 lemon (zest and juice)
- 1 bunch basil, chopped
- Salt to taste
- 2 cups water

## Instructions

1. In a deep pan or pot, heat olive oil with garlic and red chile. Sauté 1 minute.
2. Add the potatoes, then the water, rapini, and salt. Cook 20 minutes, until potatoes are soft and the water has reduced by half.
3. Smash about a quarter of the potatoes to thicken the dish. Zest the lemon over the top.
4. Off the heat, rest 20 minutes.
5. Before serving, stir in basil and a squeeze of lemon juice.
6. Serve with toasted Italian bread.
