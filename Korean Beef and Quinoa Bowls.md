# Korean Beef and Quinoa Bowls

**Serves:** 4 · **Time:** 15 min

## Ingredients

- 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1/4 tsp ground ginger
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 lb lean ground beef
- 1/4 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- 2 green onions, thinly sliced

## Instructions

1. Combine quinoa with 2 cups water (or chicken stock) in a small pot. Bring to a boil, reduce to a simmer, cover, and cook 15 minutes.
2. Heat oil in a large pan. Brown the ground beef, then drain most of the fat. Push to the side and cook the garlic and ginger about 1 minute, until fragrant.
3. Whisk together brown sugar, soy sauce, sesame oil, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper.
4. Add the sauce to the beef and cook 3–5 minutes.
5. Fluff quinoa with a fork. Serve beef over quinoa, topped with green onion.
