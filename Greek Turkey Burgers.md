# Greek Turkey Burgers

**Makes:** 5 burgers

## Ingredients

### Lemon dill yogurt sauce

- 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
- 1/2 tbsp lemon juice
- 1/8 tsp garlic powder
- 1/8 tsp dried dill
- 1/8 tsp salt

### Turkey burgers

- 19 oz ground turkey (93% lean)
- 1/4 cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes
- 1/4 cup minced red onion
- 2 oz frozen spinach (~1 cup frozen, ~1/4 cup thawed and squeezed dry)
- 1/4 cup crumbled feta
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1/4 tsp garlic powder
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp cooking oil

### To serve

- 5 hamburger buns
- 1/2 cucumber, sliced
- Sliced red onion

## Instructions

1. Stir yogurt, lemon juice, garlic powder, dill, and salt. Refrigerate.
2. Thaw the spinach and squeeze out all the moisture.
3. In a bowl, mix turkey, sun-dried tomatoes, red onion, feta, spinach, oregano, garlic powder, and salt until evenly combined.
4. Shape into 5 patties — flatter and wider than you think, since they shrink as they cook.
5. Heat 1/2 tbsp oil in a skillet over medium. Cook 3 patties 5–7 minutes per side, until browned and cooked through. Repeat with remaining oil and patties.
6. Smear yogurt sauce on each bun. Add a patty, cucumber, and red onion.
