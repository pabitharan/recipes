# Halal Cart White Sauce

**Serves:** 8 · **Time:** 5 min

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup sour cream
- 1/3 cup mayonnaise
- 1 tbsp white vinegar
- 2 tsp water
- 2 tsp lemon juice
- 1/2–2 tsp sugar
- 1 tsp dried parsley
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 1/4 tsp kosher salt

## Instructions

1. Whisk all ingredients in a medium bowl, starting with 1/2 tsp sugar. Don't over-whisk or the sauce will thin out.
2. Taste and add more sugar as needed, a little at a time.
