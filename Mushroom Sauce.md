# Creamy Mushroom Sauce

**Makes:** ~3 cups · **Time:** 20 min

## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp unsalted butter
- 1/2 tbsp olive oil
- 4 cups sliced mushrooms (about 10 oz)
- Pinch of salt and pepper
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 cup dry white wine or rosé
- 1/2 cup chicken or vegetable broth
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1/2 cup finely grated parmesan
- 2 tsp fresh thyme leaves (or 1/4–1/2 tsp dried)

## Instructions

1. Heat oil and butter in a skillet over medium-high. Add mushrooms and cook until golden brown, about 4–5 minutes. No need to stir constantly.
2. Add garlic with a pinch of salt and pepper. Cook about 1 minute, until garlic is golden.
3. Add wine. Stir, scraping the pan, for 1 minute, until mostly evaporated.
4. Add broth, cream, and parmesan. Stir and lower heat to medium so the sauce simmers gently — don't let it boil rapidly.
5. Simmer 2–3 minutes until thickened. It should be rich, not super thick.
6. Stir in thyme. Adjust salt and pepper. Serve over steak, chicken, pasta, or baked potatoes.
