# Pasta con Ricotta

**Serves:** 4

## Ingredients

- 1 (14 oz) package fusilli (or other short pasta)
- 3/4 cup ricotta
- 8–10 marinated sun-dried tomatoes
- 6–7 basil leaves
- 1 jalapeño, sliced
- 1 cup grape tomatoes
- 1 tsp salt
- 4 tbsp olive oil

## Instructions

1. Cook pasta in salted water.
2. While the pasta cooks, blend ricotta, sun-dried tomatoes, basil, jalapeño, grape tomatoes, salt, and olive oil into a smooth sauce.
3. About 1 minute before the pasta is al dente, add it to the sauce along with 1 cup of pasta water.
4. Cook together until the pasta is al dente and the sauce has thickened.
