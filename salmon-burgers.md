# Salmon Burgers

**Serves:** 6 · **Prep:** 30 min · **Cook:** 10 min

## Ingredients

### Caper mayonnaise

- 3/4 cup mayonnaise
- 4 tsp lemon juice
- 4 tsp capers, chopped
- 1 small clove garlic, finely chopped

### Salmon patties

- 1 1/2 lbs skinless salmon, finely chopped
- 2 green onions, finely chopped
- 1/3 cup breadcrumbs
- 1 egg, lightly beaten
- 2 tbsp chopped parsley
- 2 tbsp mayonnaise
- 2 tsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp dry mustard
- Salt and pepper

### To serve

- 6 hamburger buns
- 6 leaves lettuce
- 1 English cucumber, thinly sliced lengthwise

## Instructions

1. **Caper mayo:** Stir all ingredients together. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
2. Preheat the grill on high. Oil the grate.
3. **Patties:** Combine all patty ingredients until smooth. Add more breadcrumbs if too soft. Season with salt and pepper. Shape into 6 patties.
4. Lower grill to medium. Grill patties 3–4 minutes per side, until cooked through. Toast the buns.
5. Spread caper mayo on the buns. Layer lettuce, patty, and cucumber.
