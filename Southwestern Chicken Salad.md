# Southwestern Chicken Salad

**Serves:** 6 · **Time:** 10 min

## Ingredients

### Salad

- 6 cups chopped romaine
- 6 cups cubed cooked chicken (~1 lb cooked)
- 1 1/2 cups corn (canned, frozen and thawed, or fresh)
- 1 1/2 cups black beans, drained and rinsed
- 2 tomatoes, diced
- 1 avocado, diced
- 1/2 red onion, diced
- 3/4 cup shredded cheddar
- Handful chopped cilantro
- 1 cup crushed tortilla chips

### Dressing

- 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
- 3 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
- 2 tsp honey
- 2 tsp apple cider vinegar
- 1 tsp minced garlic
- Juice of 1 lime
- 1/2 jalapeño, finely diced
- 2 heaping tsp taco seasoning
- Salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Whisk all dressing ingredients in a small bowl.
2. Combine all salad ingredients except tortilla chips in a large bowl.
3. Pour over the dressing and toss.
4. Top with tortilla chips just before serving so they stay crunchy.

**Make ahead:** Prepare through step 2 a day in advance — the flavors deepen overnight. Add dressing and chips when serving.
