# Lemony Artichoke and Quinoa Salad

**Serves:** 4

## Ingredients

- 1 cup quinoa
- 1 fresh lemon (zest and juice)
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp salt
- Freshly cracked pepper
- 1 (13 oz) can quartered artichoke hearts
- 1 red bell pepper, finely diced
- 1 cup chopped fresh parsley
- 1 (15 oz) can chickpeas, rinsed and drained

## Instructions

1. Rinse quinoa in a fine sieve. Combine with 1 3/4 cups water in a pot, cover, bring to a boil, then reduce to low and simmer 15 minutes.
2. Make the dressing: zest the lemon, then squeeze the juice (you want 1/4–1/3 cup). In a bowl or jar, combine lemon juice, 1 tsp zest, olive oil, garlic, salt, and 10–15 cranks of pepper. Whisk or shake to combine.
3. Spread cooked quinoa in a wide shallow dish and refrigerate uncovered about 15 minutes to cool.
4. Drain and roughly chop the artichoke hearts. Combine artichokes, bell pepper, parsley, and chickpeas in a large bowl.
5. Add cooled quinoa. Toss gently. Drizzle with dressing and toss again. Serve immediately or refrigerate up to 4–5 days.
