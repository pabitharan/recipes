# Grilled Chicken Chop Salad

**Serves:** 6 · **Time:** 45 min

## Ingredients

### Shawarma chicken

- 6 boneless chicken thighs
- Salt
- 2 tbsp Kewpie mayo
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp ground turmeric
- 1 tsp sumac
- 1 tsp black pepper
- Pinch of ground cinnamon
- Pinch of cayenne

### Yogurt sauce

- 2/3 cup plain non-fat yogurt
- 2 cloves garlic, grated
- 1 tsp black pepper
- Juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1 tsp minced fresh dill
- Salt to taste

### Salad

- Romaine lettuce, chopped
- Cherry tomatoes, halved
- Cucumber, cubed
- Feta, crumbled
- Pickled onions
- Tortilla strips or pita chips

## Instructions

1. **Marinate the chicken.** Salt thighs all over. Mix the spices with the mayo, then rub all over the chicken.
2. **Sear the chicken.** On a ripping-hot griddle or cast iron, sear the chicken on both sides until well browned and cooked to ~170°F internal. Rest before slicing.
3. **Make the dressing.** Stir together yogurt, garlic, pepper, lemon juice, and dill. Taste and adjust salt.
4. **Assemble.** Lettuce in the bowl, then tortilla strips or pita, then yogurt sauce, cucumber, tomato, feta, sliced chicken, and pickled onion.
