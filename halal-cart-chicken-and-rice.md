# Halal Cart-Style Chicken and Rice with White Sauce

**Serves:** 4 · **Prep:** 15 min · **Cook:** 40 min · **Marinate:** 1+ hr

## Ingredients

### Chicken

- 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
- 1 tbsp chopped fresh oregano
- 1/2 tsp ground coriander
- 3 cloves garlic, roughly chopped (~1 1/2 tbsp)
- 1/4 cup light olive oil
- Kosher salt and pepper
- 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs (6–8 thighs), trimmed
- 1 tbsp vegetable or canola oil

### Rice

- 2 tbsp unsalted butter
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1/4 tsp ground cumin
- 1 1/2 cups long-grain or basmati rice
- 2 1/2 cups chicken broth
- Kosher salt and pepper

### White sauce

- 1/2 cup mayonnaise
- 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 2 tbsp white vinegar
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
- 2 tsp black pepper
- Kosher salt

### To serve

- 1 head iceberg lettuce, shredded
- 1 large tomato, in wedges
- Pocketless pita, brushed with butter, toasted, and cut into 1×3-inch strips
- Harissa-style hot sauce

## Instructions

1. **Marinate.** Blend lemon juice, oregano, coriander, garlic, and olive oil until smooth. Season with salt and pepper. Place chicken in a gallon zip-top bag with half the marinade (reserve the rest in the fridge). Marinate 1–4 hours, turning occasionally.
2. **Sear.** Pat chicken dry. Season generously with salt and pepper, going heavy on the pepper. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a 12-inch heavy skillet over medium-high until lightly smoking. Sear chicken without moving until lightly browned, about 4 minutes. Flip, reduce to medium, and cook until 165°F internal, about 6 minutes. Rest 5 minutes on a cutting board.
3. Roughly chop chicken into 1/4–1/2-inch chunks. Transfer to a bowl, add the reserved marinade, cover, and refrigerate while you cook the rice.
4. **Rice.** Melt butter in a Dutch oven over medium. Add turmeric and cumin and cook about 1 minute, until fragrant. Add rice and stir to coat. Cook, stirring often, about 4 minutes, until lightly toasted. Add broth, season, bring to a boil. Cover, reduce to a simmer, and cook 15 minutes undisturbed. Off the heat, rest covered 15 minutes.
5. **Sauce.** Whisk mayo, yogurt, sugar, vinegar, lemon juice, parsley, and 2 tsp black pepper. Salt to taste.
6. **Finish.** Return chicken with marinade and juices to the skillet. Cook over medium-high, stirring, until heated through. Serve over rice with lettuce, tomato, and pita. Top with white sauce and hot sauce.

**Tip:** Don't marinate longer than 4 hours or the chicken texture goes mushy.
