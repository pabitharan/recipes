# Mediterranean Tuna Salad

**Serves:** 6 (about 1 cup each) · **Time:** 20 min

## Ingredients

### Vinaigrette

- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp freshly cracked black pepper

### Salad

- 1 cucumber (English or regular), cut into quarter rounds
- 1 pint grape tomatoes, halved
- 1/2 red onion, finely diced
- 1/4 bunch fresh parsley, chopped (~1/2 cup)
- 1 (15 oz) can cannellini beans, rinsed and drained
- 1 (12 oz) can chunk light tuna, drained
- 2 oz feta, crumbled

## Instructions

1. Whisk all vinaigrette ingredients in a bowl or jar. Set aside.
2. *Optional:* To soften the bite of the red onion, slice it and soak in ice water 5 minutes before dicing.
3. Combine cucumber, tomato, onion, and parsley in a bowl. Pour over the vinaigrette and stir to coat.
4. Add beans, tuna, and feta. Fold gently — you want the tuna in larger flakes, not broken up.
5. Serve immediately or refrigerate up to 4 days. Stir before serving to redistribute the dressing.
