# Autumn Kale and Sweet Potato Salad

**Serves:** 4

## Ingredients

### Roasted sweet potatoes

- 2 lbs sweet potatoes
- 1 tbsp cooking oil
- 1/4 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp ground cumin
- 1/4 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/4 tsp salt

### Maple tahini dressing

- 1/4 cup tahini
- 1/4 cup water
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 tbsp maple syrup
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp garlic powder
- 1/8 tsp cayenne

### Salad

- 6 cups chopped kale (about 1/2 bunch)
- 1 large sweet apple (e.g. Honeycrisp or Gala)
- 1/4 cup pepitas
- 1/4 cup chopped pecans
- 1/4 cup dried cranberries

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Peel and dice sweet potatoes into 1/2-inch pieces. Toss on a parchment-lined sheet pan with oil, cinnamon, cumin, smoked paprika, and salt.
2. Roast about 30 minutes, stirring halfway, until tender and browned at the edges.
3. Whisk or blend dressing ingredients until smooth.
4. Strip kale leaves from stems, rinse, drain, and slice into thin strips. Divide between four bowls.
5. Quarter and core the apple, slice thin, and add to each bowl.
6. Top each bowl with 1 tbsp each of pepitas, pecans, and cranberries. Add the cooled sweet potatoes. Dress and serve, or store dressing separately for up to 5 days.
